Chicken Soup Recipe

It's hard to go wrong with this one, especially on a rainy day.

Chicken soup is one of those classic dishes that you need to include as one of your top recipes.
It's easy to portion out and equally tasty. Let's get into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Make broth
  2. Cook vegetables
  3. Strain the broth
  4. Shred the chicken
  5. Simmer and serve!
