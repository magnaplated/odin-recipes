Steak Recipe

This recipe has been a personal favorite of mine. It's sure to make a delicious dinner!

Making steak is often seen as a difficult task. I'm here to dispel this myth. All you need to make good steak is a handful of ingredients, a pan, and some patience!

Everything you need can be found in your local supermarket for a fair price. Without further ado, here's the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of sirloin steak
  • 1 tbsp coarse salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Remove packaging
  2. Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon of salt onto pan
  3. Turn heat to high on pan
  4. Place steak onto pan
  5. Let steak sizzle for 1 minute, then flip over
  6. Continue to cook on high heat, flipping steak every 30 seconds
  7. When steak has developed a dark brown crust, check for doneness
  8. Remove steak to cutting board and tent lightly with foil
  9. Enjoy!
